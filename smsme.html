SMS Me

SMSMe is a mobile application based on text messages. SMSMe was born after analysing the need of people to find places, services, maybe Uber cars and what not. The real problem is that many of those people have no connectivity to data, or internet. Morever, if we think that in third world countries, having connectivity to the internet is more a luxury than a necessity, SMSMe aims to become your personal assistant without using a single byte from your internet connection.

SMSMe was developed during McHacks, McGill University Hackathon in Montreal, Quebec. Although SMSMe didn't win in McHacks, it made it to the top 10! And it is marked as winner in DevPost!

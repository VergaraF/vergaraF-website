Mafia Casino

Mafia Casino is a video game for mobile devices, android and iOS. Mafia Casino is initially a board game created by Boite de jeux, but the video game (app) was then developed by Beam Me Up Games, in Montreal, Quebec, Canada.
I joined the developing team as an intern during winter 2015 until end of spring. I was mainly in charged of assisting the developing team of the game, by creating bonus features that were not implemented in the game yet, fixing bugs (debugging), and refactoring. However, I sucessfully went beyond boundaries (with given permission) and re-implemented main features of the game, such as a new rewarding system. I personally reviewed functions and code, and improved their implementation following good programming practices. By making good code, great code.

Mafia Casino allowed me to explore the game industry, and also allowed me to explore production at a bigger scale than school and side projects had allowed me to.

Daniel Beaudin - Project Manager

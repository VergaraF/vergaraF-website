Touber

Touber is a web application. Students recently admitted to College may come from different countries, cities or states; Touber solves the problem of getting to know campus by creating a service of tour guides to students. Its objective was to partner up with admissions to let newly admitted students know about the service. Students may be able to contact a tour guide and offer him an optional incentive for him to walk them around Univerity Campus. Its other objective was to change the way turists interact with their destinations by offering a local person the opportunity to take them to the best places of a city. The social feature of Touber was to make it possible to see live tours on a map, using Google Maps, so people could join them.

Touber was developed during HackHarvard, Harvard University Hackathon in Cambridge, Massachusetts (US). Although Touber didn't win in HackHarvard, this was my first hackathon and I got to love the hackathon community!.

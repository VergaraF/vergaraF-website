About me!

Hello! My name is Fabian Vergara and I am a Software Engineering student at Concordia University, in Montreal, with emphasis in web services and applications. I am aiming to become a full-stack developer: from how data is handled and processed in the backend, to all the way to the UI/UX in the frontend. I don't exclude any branch in the development process of software. I've always seen myself as a dedicated person who loves knowledge, and also loves to apply that knowledge to relevant things. My career goal is to do something relevant that somehow could improve and have an impact people's lives; as minimal as it could be. I enjoy participating in hackathons and coding side projects in my free time. I also enjoy photography and travelling!

I finished my studies in Computer Science at Vanier College, in Montreal. My studies at Vanier granted me a technical DEC (Diplome des Etudes Collegiales) which should compare to an associate degree in places outside Quebec. I am doing my bachelor at Concordia University to complete my education and knowledge.
